# NJ School Student-Support Staff Finder

This is a local website that helps find publicly listed emails for school counselors, psychologists, social workers, therapists, and similar staff at New Jersey schools.

You just paste in school names, one per line. The app automatically finds the school district, looks for the official school or district website, and searches for relevant staff.

It only reports emails that are actually shown publicly on an official school or district website. It does not guess emails.
